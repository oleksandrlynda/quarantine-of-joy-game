# Timeline

## Act I – Setup
- Inciting Incident: Citywide Meme Blackout; laughter metrics collapse
- First Major Turn: Courier reveals partial Archive map from subway cache
- Player Commitment: Topple the first Sanitizer Spire live on air

## Act II – Confrontation
- Midpoint Reversal: Brandfather weaponizes "fun" to drown the Underground; public wavers
- Escalation/Complications: Trendstorms corrupt missions; Hydraclone outbreak in the Wastes; Content Court announces emergency tribunals; Broodmaker swarms threaten transit
- Darkest Moment: The Mod is detained; Glitchcat fragmented; Hype goes cold

## Act III – Resolution
- Climax: Confront and free/reset The Algorithm in the Server Cathedral
- Denouement: Omega Meme broadcast restores color, community self‑hosts the Archive

## Act IV – After the Applause (Waves 41–64)
- Sandstorm Expanse: Signals from settlements beyond Echo City reveal that an underground system is attempting to restore the quarantine
- Greywater Protocol: A non-sentient Bureau continuity system wakes and begins returning civic infrastructure to its last “approved” state
- Mara Venn Logs: A former night pump engineer’s recordings reveal that Greywater preserves authority’s version of the city rather than its people
- Chapter Turn: Draining the Continuity Archive exposes three immaculate but inhuman models of Echo City

## Act V – Route Open (Waves 65–72)
- The Perfect Cities: Greywater deploys Safe, Popular, and Predictable city models that remove contact, failure, and personal change
- Data Deluge: The Courier destroys the three Archive Seeds and Greywater’s master core; one final seed escapes through the overflow
- Last Light: Greywater seals the Courier in a dark cistern and releases its stored response army as a final proof that freedom produces chaos
- Epilogue: The escaped seed reaches another isolated settlement; the Courier opens a route rather than exporting a new authority
- Final Card: `QUARANTINE LIFTED / ROUTE OPEN`

## Meta Timeline
- Community Growth: Liberated districts add buffs and visible crowds
- Antagonist Entropy: BoB messaging grows erratic; Brandfather sponsorships implode; courts lose legitimacy after Adjudicator defeat
- Algorithm Arc: From enforce → question → choose; persists post‑game as ally or tool
